Hi, I'm Yuhan Ye :)

Originally from Xiamen, a beautiful costal city in southern China. I studied Graphic Design Communication at Chelsea College of Arts (UAL), and I'm now pursuing Master in Design Studies at Harvard Graduate School of Design (GSD). I'm still figuring things out as I go, but for now, I'm passionate about multimedia art, often working across installation, film, printed publication and photography.
